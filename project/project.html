Welcome to GC INC. – Goncharov Construction Inc.

When you build with Goncharov Construction, you can count on the backing of an experienced team who are dedicated to a standard of excellence in their profession. You can take comfort in knowing that all the major details of your project will be taken care of.

We are licensed builders through Saskatoon Land and backed by the Saskatchewan New Home Warranty program.

Our goal is to make your home building experience go as smoothly as possible. We’ll take care of all major details so that you can get on with enjoying life. Our experienced team is dedicated to delivering the highest quality on time, and on budget.

At GC INC. we want every job to represent our best work; from kitchen, bathroom, and basement renovations to additions and new home builds.

About Us
